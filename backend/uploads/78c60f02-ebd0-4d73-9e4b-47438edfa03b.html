Restoring Hope, Rebuilding Lives, Raising Generations.

At YUVA FOUNDATION, our heart beats for the forgotten, the broken, and the voiceless. We work with children who have no access to education, women who have lost all hope, youth who are misled and confused, and the elderly who are left alone. Our mission is simple: to love, to care, and to build lives with dignity and hope.

About Us

YUVA FOUNDATION is a non-profit, non-political, and humanitarian organization founded with a vision to uplift the underprivileged and give them a meaningful life.

We strongly believe that every person matters. A child begging on the street, a widow abandoned by her family, a youth caught in addiction, or an elderly person left alone—all deserve love, respect, and a chance for a better tomorrow.

Through education, skill development, care homes, rehabilitation, and volunteer partnerships, we are creating a movement of compassion that is transforming lives in Telangana and beyond.

What We Do

Child Education & Care

We provide free education, school materials, and holistic development programs.

Volunteer & Partnership Programs

We welcome local and foreign volunteers and partner with corporates, institutions, and individuals.

Women Empowerment

We provide skill training in tailoring, handicrafts, and computer skills so women can earn a living.

Orphanages & Old Age Homes

Our homes provide shelter, food, education, healthcare, and above all—family.

Rehabilitation Centres

We help individuals break free from addictions and destructive lifestyles.

Reaching the Unreached

We serve remote villages lacking education, healthcare, and livelihood support.

Our Impact

These are not just numbers; they are lives transformed.

A 7-year-old boy who used to beg at bus stands is now in school and dreams of becoming a police officer.

A widow trained in tailoring now runs her own shop and educates her children.

An elderly man, once abandoned, now smiles every morning surrounded by love and care in our old age home.

👉 Every story is written because someone cared. You can be that someone today.
